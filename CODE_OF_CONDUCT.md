# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a welcoming and respectful experience for everyone.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be kind and constructive in comments and reviews
- Respect differing viewpoints and experience levels
- Accept feedback gracefully

## Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately unhelpful comments

## Enforcement

Unacceptable behaviour can be reported by opening an issue or contacting the maintainer. Reports will be reviewed and may result in a ban from the project.
